Customize Your PC

Use PCPartPicker to design your dream machine. Once you’ve created your build, send us the link using the form below — we’ll review it and get back to you with a quote, recommendations, or next steps.

Launch PC Builder

Instructions

  • Click “Launch PC Builder” to open PCPartPicker.
  • Select the parts you want in your build.
  • Click “Save As,” and uncheck “Private.”
  • Right-click your saved build and copy the link (not the “Share” button link).
  • Paste it into the form below and submit.
