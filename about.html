Who We Are

James Industries is a veteran-owned IT services company based in Indiana, focused on providing reliable, honest, and affordable tech support to individuals, families, and small businesses.

Our mission is simple: deliver practical, trustworthy solutions — whether you need a custom PC build, malware removal, network setup, diagnostics, or general tech support. We approach every project with integrity, attention to detail, and genuine care for the people we serve.

We stand behind the same values lived in the Marine Corps: honor, commitment, discipline, and taking care of those who trust us.

What We Do

  • PC repair, cleanup, optimization, and upgrades
  • Virus and malware removal
  • Home network setup and troubleshooting
  • Custom gaming and workstation PC builds
  • Tech consultations and diagnostics
  • Web design and development for small businesses

Who I Am


Dale James — Founder & Lead Technician

My name is Dale James, and I founded James Industries out of a passion for technology and a desire to help people. I'm a former Marine, a follower of Jesus Christ, and a lifelong tech enthusiast. I love building computers, designing websites, writing code, and solving real-world tech problems.

I’m also a husband and father of three boys. For now, I work as a truck driver while building this business — but my dream is to make James Industries my full-time mission and career. After years of trying to break into tech through traditional paths, I chose to build something of my own instead.

Thank you for visiting our site. I look forward to earning your trust and your business.
