Who We Are

James Industries is a veteran-owned IT services company based in Indiana. We are committed to providing reliable and affordable tech support to individuals and small businesses in our community.

Our mission is simple: to deliver honest, practical, and professional solutions for every tech challenge. Whether it's a custom PC build, malware removal, network setup, or a tech consultation — we bring the knowledge and dedication to get the job done right.

We’re proud to serve with the same values we lived by in the military: integrity, attention to detail, and putting people first.
